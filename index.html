About Us

We've been making healthy food since a decade.

The Ichiraku Ramen of Manali, holds a cherished place in the history of India's Tourism industry. Established in 1974 by Master Jiraiya. At The Ichiraku Ramen, our mission is to provide guests with an unforgettable experience that celebrates the rich heritage and traditions of India, while offering world-class eatery and services.
